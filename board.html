Faculty Advisors

Dr. Snigdha Khuntia

In science and engineering, a good advisor seeks to help a student optimize an educational experience to assist their disciplinary culture. For us this figure is Dr. Snigdha Khuntia, PhD from IIT Guwahati. Her areas of interest are Advanced Oxidation Processes, Waste Water Treatment, Biosorption, Adsorption and Heavy Metal removal from waste water.

Dr. Arijit Ganguli

Professor Ganguli received his PhD from the Institute of Chemical Technology, Mumbai. He has more than eight years of experience in the chemical industry and has worked on projects spanning computational fluid dynamics of multiphase flows, process modelling and some commodity chemical processes. He has about 18 international publications in such journals as Chemical Engineering Science, Chemical Engineering Research and Design, American Insitute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE), and Physics of Fluids. He has already amassed 250 citations to his publications.
