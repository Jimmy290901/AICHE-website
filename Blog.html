Chem E-car

Chem-E-Car works as a student-oriented team, where the acquaintance will implement their engineering skills to design optimized cars. This will enhance the ability of the student to think creatively and productively to achieve optimal solutions. Teamwork will unite the members to work together on a mundane vision. Moreover, they will gain the experience to work on a project solely. “WE TOGETHER STRONG TOGETHER GROW TOGETHER” – our slogan reflects the denotation of working together. This competition provides an individual to convey their expertise on the next level. They will get the opportunity to implement their classroom knowledge beyond the walls into the real world.

Properties of Chem E-car
  • The car works on electrical energy obtained from electrochemical reaction between potassium hydroxide solution, magnesium strip and graphite rod.
  • Cells are connected in various combinations as per requirement to run the car up to specific distance.
  • The output from the combination of cells is connected to motors for rotating the wheels.
  • For the stopping mechanism, the Iodine clock is used.
